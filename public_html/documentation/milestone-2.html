Content Strategy

My website will be structured in the following manner

Story

This section will introduce Isaac to the world. I will include a photo of him in front of the food truck and give a little background story of the food truck.

Menu

The menu will be a carousel of photos of the food. I have opted for a format that will showcase the food. The photo will be first and then under it a description and price. I strongly believe that when ordering it is very important for people to see what they are getting.

Contact Us

This section will allow clients to reach out if they have questions about the food or if they want to hire the food truck for a catering event. This is also where the social media links will be for further engagement.

Color scheme

The color scheme if the website will be black and yellow with red. I know it will be a difficult task to make it accessible and aesthetically pleasing, but I want to stick to the color of the food truck. Since I know the colors are a lot I will keep the website minimal with clean lines.
